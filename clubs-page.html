Get Engaged

Greendale University is home for more than 20 student organisations where the student fraternity engage in various activities from seminars and workshops to galas and musical nights. The main student body is the Student Union which focuses mainly about student welfare. There are other student organisations such as Young Innovators Club, Creative and Performing Arts Society, Model United Nations and Media Unit of Greendale University

Student Union

The GDU student union represents the interests and needs of their peers in the university. They organize various projects and activities to enhance the academic, social and cultural life of the students. Some of the projects they do include advocating for student rights and welfare on issues such as tuition fees, academic policies, mental health and diversity, providing services and resources such as financial aid, legal advice, health care and career guidance, running clubs and societies that cater to different hobbies, passions and causes and hosting events and campaigns that promote awareness, engagement and fun among the student community.

GDU student union is focused on student welfare and aims to create a positive and supportive environment for everyone to learn, grow and thrive.

Young Inventors Club

The young inventors club is focused on innovation and aims to foster a culture of curiosity, experimentation and excellence among the students. Memebers of this club share a passion for innovation and creativity in university. They inspire and challenge each other to come up with new ideas and solutions for various problems and opportunities. The year of these young inovators includes providing workshops and mentorship on topics such as design thinking, prototyping, entrepreneurship and intellectual property, organizing competitions and hackathons that encourage students to showcase their inventions and win prizes and recognition, collaborating with industry partners and experts who offer feedback, guidance and support for their projects and creating a network and community of like-minded peers who motivate and inspire each other to pursue their dreams

Creative and Performing Arts Society

The Creative and Performing Arts Society (CPAS) is a student-led organization that aims to foster and showcase the artistic talents of the university community. CPAS organizes various events and activities throughout the year, such as workshops, exhibitions, competitions, and performances. CPAS welcomes students from all disciplines and backgrounds who are interested in exploring and expressing their creativity through different forms of art, such as music, dance, drama, poetry, painting, photography, and more. CPAS also provides opportunities for students to network with professionals and peers in the creative and performing arts industry. CPAS is a vibrant and inclusive society that celebrates diversity and culture through art

Model United Nations

The Model United Nations Club is a student-led organization that aims to foster global awareness, diplomatic skills, and civic engagement among university students. The club participates in various regional and national conferences, where members represent different countries and debate on current world issues. The club also organizes workshops, guest lectures, and social events to enhance the learning experience and build a sense of community.Whether you are a beginner or an experienced delegate, the Model United Nations Club will provide you with the support and guidance you need to succeed in MUN conferences and beyond. Don’t miss this chance to make a difference in the world and have fun at the same time. The club welcomes students from all majors and backgrounds who are interested in international affairs, public speaking, and leadership. Join us today and become a part of the global MUN community!

Media Unit of Greendale University

The Media Unit is a creative and innovative team that provides media services and solutions to the university community. The Media Unit offers a range of services, such as video production and editing, photography and graphic design, podcasting and audio recording, web design and development and social media management and marketing. The Media Unit works closely with various departments, faculties, and student groups to produce high-quality and engaging content that showcases the university’s achievements, events, and stories. The Media Unit also supports the university’s teaching and learning objectives by providing media resources and training to students and staff. The Media Unit is always looking for new ideas and collaborations to enhance the university’s media presence and reputation. Join us today and let us help you with your passion.
