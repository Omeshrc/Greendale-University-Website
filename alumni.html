Welcome to Greendale Alumni!

At Greendale University, we believe that education is a lifelong journey, and our alumni are the living testament to the transformative power of knowledge. Whether you recently graduated or are a seasoned alumnus, this platform is designed to reconnect you with your alma mater and foster a vibrant community of like-minded individuals.

Our alumni community spans the globe, representing a diverse array of professions and industries. As a Greendale graduate, you are part of a network that stretches far and wide, offering endless opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Reunions 2023 was an absolute blast, and we couldn't have asked for a more picture-perfect celebration of our beloved alma mater. Thank you to each and every one of our 25,000 alumni, friends, and family members who returned to make this event truly special.

Mark your Calenders!

After a four-year hiatus due to the unprecedented challenges posed by the Covid-19 pandemic, we are excited to welcome our esteemed alumni back for a special and long-overdue gathering.
