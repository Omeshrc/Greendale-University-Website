About us

The Greendale University is a private university that provides flexible and affordable education to students from all over the world. It is headquartered in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Greendale University was founded in 2020 with the aim of making higher education accessible and convenient for everyone. We have a vision to be a leading university that empowers learners to achieve their personal and professional goals. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in various disciplines and fields of study.
