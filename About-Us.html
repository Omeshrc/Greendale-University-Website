Get to Know About us

The Greendale University is a private university that provides flexible and affordable education to students from all over the world. It is headquartered in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Greendale University was founded in 2020 with the aim of making higher education accessible and convenient for everyone. We have a vision to be a leading university that empowers learners to achieve their personal and professional goals.

We have four faculties with 16 academic departments. The faculties are Business and Management, Engineering and Technology, Humanities and Social Sciences, and Health and Life Sciences. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in various disciplines and fields of study. The programs are delivered through an innovative online learning platform that allows students to study at their own pace and place. The programs are accredited by recognized agencies and organizations such as the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP), the Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology (ABET), the Council for Accreditation of Counseling and Related Educational Programs (CACREP), and the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE).

We also offer several online courses that are open to anyone who wants to learn new skills or enhance their knowledge. The online courses cover topics such as web development, programming, digital marketing, entrepreneurship, leadership, project management, and more. The online courses are self-paced and interactive, and provide certificates upon completion.

Greendale University has a strong commitment towards research and innovation. The faculty members and students in various disciplines engage in cutting-edge research that addresses global challenges and creates social impact. The university has several research centers and units that facilitate interdisciplinary and collaborative research projects. The university also publishes several academic journals that disseminate the research findings and scholarly contributions of its faculty members and students.

We as a university has established many international collaborations and partnerships with reputed universities and organizations around the world. The university is a member of the United Nations Academic Impact (UNAI) and the International Association of Universities (IAU). We also have bilateral agreements with several foreign universities for student and staff exchange programs and joint degrees. Greendale University also provides opportunities for students to participate in sports and co-curricular activities that enhance their physical, mental, and social well-being. The university has a sports club that organizes online tournaments and competitions in various sports such as chess, e-sports, fitness, yoga, and more. We also have a student union that represents the interests and welfare of the students. The student union organizes events and activities such as webinars, workshops, quizzes, debates, cultural festivals, social clubs, and more.
